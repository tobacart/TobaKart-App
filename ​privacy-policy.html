Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to TobaKart. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. Operating within the tobacco industry for over nine years, we adhere to strict standards to safeguard the trust you place in us when using our platform for local shopping in Surat.

2. Data Collection

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the platform, express an interest in obtaining information about our products, or otherwise contact us. This includes:

  • Contact Data: Full name, phone number, email address, and delivery address.
  • Account Data: Securely stored credentials and age verification documents required by law.
  • Transaction Data: Order history, preferred delivery times, and UPI transaction references (UTRs).

3. How We Use Your Information

We process your information for purposes based on legitimate business interests, the fulfillment of our contract with you, and compliance with our legal obligations. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Facilitate account creation and logon processes.
  • Fulfill and manage your orders, payments, and returns.
  • Deliver targeted marketing and promotional communications (if opted-in).
  • Verify age compliance strictly required for tobacco industry purchases.

4. Data Protection & Security

We implement enterprise-grade security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process. While we employ industry-standard encryption and secure local storage methods, please remember that no transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Third-Party Services

TobaKart integrates with certain third-party services to enhance your experience, such as payment gateways (UPI) and map APIs for address location. These third parties have access to your personal information only to perform specific tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy notice from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Last Updated" date and the revised version will be effective as soon as it is accessible. We encourage you to review this policy frequently.

7. Contact Details

If you have questions or comments about this notice, you may email us at support@tobacart.com or visit our Contact Us page.
