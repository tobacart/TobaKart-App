Cookie Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

1. What Are Cookies & Local Storage?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. In addition to standard cookies, modern web applications like TobaKart utilize Web Storage (Local Storage) to save data directly on your device. This technology allows our Progressive Web App (PWA) to function rapidly and seamlessly without constantly querying a server.

2. How We Use These Technologies

Operating as a fast, client-side application, we rely on Local Storage and essential cookies for the following core functionalities:

  • Authentication (Strictly Necessary): Storing your active user session (`tobacart_user_auth`) so you do not have to log in every time you open the app.
  • Cart Management (Strictly Necessary): Keeping your selected items in the cart (`cart_[userId]`) even if you close the browser or navigate away.
  • Personalization (Functional): Remembering your chosen app language (English, Gujarati, Hindi, or Marathi) and tracking category preferences (`tobacart_user_prefs`) to tailor your "Instant Buy" recommendations.
  • Offline Capability: Caching essential product data (`tobacart_products`) locally to ensure the app loads instantly, even on slow network connections in Surat.

3. Third-Party Cookies

We utilize Google Firebase to securely sync data (like orders and inventory) across devices. Firebase may set its own cookies or tracking tokens to manage secure database connections, monitor performance, and prevent fraudulent activity. These are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Managing Your Preferences

Because the majority of our data is stored via Local Storage rather than traditional cookies, the most effective way to clear your data is to use your browser's "Clear Browsing Data" or "Clear Site Data" tools. Warning: Clearing Local Storage will sign you out of your TobaKart guest or registered account and empty your shopping cart.

5. Your Consent

By continuing to use the TobaKart platform, you consent to our use of cookies and Local Storage as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue the use of our services and clear your browser cache.
