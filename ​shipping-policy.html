Shipping Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Delivery Areas

TobaKart currently operates as a hyper-local e-commerce platform. Our delivery services are strictly limited to designated PIN codes within Surat, Gujarat. If your PIN code falls outside our operational boundaries, you will be notified at checkout and unable to place an order.

2. Processing & Delivery Timelines

We strive to provide fast and efficient service to our local customers:

  • Order Processing: Orders are typically processed and packed within 2-4 hours of confirmation during standard business hours.
  • Standard Delivery: Delivered within 24 to 48 hours depending on order volume and your exact location in Surat.
  • Surat Local Express: Instant or same-day delivery may be available for eligible items and will be clearly indicated during checkout.

3. Delivery Charges

Delivery charges are dynamically calculated based on your exact distance from our dispatch centers and the total order value. The final shipping cost will be clearly displayed in the Cart summary before you complete your payment or confirm Cash on Delivery (COD).

4. Mandatory ID & Age Verification

Due to regulatory requirements in the tobacco industry, our delivery partners are mandated to perform strict age verification upon delivery. You must be 18 years or older to receive the package. The recipient must present a valid government-issued ID (e.g., Aadhar Card, PAN Card, Driving License) if requested. If the recipient is underage or fails to produce ID, the delivery will be canceled, and the items returned to our store without a refund of the shipping fee.

5. Secure OTP Delivery

To ensure packages are handed to the correct individual, TobaKart utilizes a secure OTP (One-Time Password) system. The OTP is generated in your app dashboard under 'My Orders'. You must share this 4-digit OTP with the delivery executive to successfully complete the handover.
