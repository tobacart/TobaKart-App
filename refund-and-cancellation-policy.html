Refund & Cancellation

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Order Cancellations

We aim to process and dispatch orders as quickly as possible. Therefore, strict cancellation windows apply:

  • Before Dispatch: You may cancel your order at any time before it has been marked as "Packed" or "Shipped" from your account dashboard.
  • After Dispatch: Once an order is handed over to our delivery partners, it cannot be canceled. If you refuse delivery, standard shipping and restocking fees may be deducted from any potential refunds.
  • Cancellation by TobaKart: We reserve the right to cancel orders due to stock unavailability, pricing errors, or failure to pass our fraud detection and age verification checks.

2. Return Policy & Non-Returnable Items

Due to the consumable nature of our products and strict health and safety standards, most items sold on TobaKart are non-returnable once delivered.

Returns will only be considered under the following exceptional circumstances:

  • You received a damaged or physically defective product.
  • You received a completely incorrect item (different from what was ordered).
  • The product has expired before the date of delivery.

3. Unboxing Video Requirement

To claim a refund or replacement for damaged, defective, or missing items, a continuous, unedited unboxing video is mandatory. The video must clearly show the sealed package, the shipping label, and the opening of the package to reveal the issue. Claims without valid video proof will be rejected to prevent fraudulent activity.

4. Refund Processing

If your cancellation or return request is approved, refunds will be initiated as follows:

  • Online Payments (UPI/Cards): Refunds will be credited back to the original source account within 5 to 7 business days, depending on your bank's processing times.
  • Cash on Delivery (COD): Refunds for COD orders that are eligible for return will be processed to a bank account or UPI ID provided by you during the return resolution process.

5. How to Request a Return or Refund

To initiate a request, please contact our Customer Care team within 24 hours of delivery. You can reach us at support@tobacart.com or via our WhatsApp support line available in the app.
